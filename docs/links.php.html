Links

  • Program Does Not Equal Program: Constraint Programming and Its Relationship to Mathematical Programming
    • PDF: The best introductory document for those interested in jOpt and its technologies. It introduces OPL, explains CP and MP and their relationship, and even mentions JS (constraint-based scheduling).

MP links

  • Computational Techniques of the Simplex Method
    • Book: Written by Istvan Maros, this book offers detailed information about linear programming problems and suggests implementations that solve them effieciently including both the primal and dual simplex algorithms.

OPL links

  • A Preview of OPL
    • PDF: An brief preview of the OPL language written by its creator, Pascal Van Hentenryck.
  • The OPL Optimization Programming Language
    • Book: The official book about OPL, also authored by Pascal Van Hentenryck.

CP links

  • Algorithms for Constraint-Satisfaction Problems: A Survey
    • PDF: A great overview of constraint satisfaction problems and solving techniques including arc consistency, backtracking, searching, variable/value ordering, etc.
  • On-line Guide to Constraint Programming by Roman Bartak
    • Website: Explains constraint satisfaction problems and includes constraint-related tutorials. Explores many of the same areas as 'Algorithms for Constraint-Satisfaction Problems: A Survey' (above).
  • Consistency Based CSP Solver
    • Website: Has a constraint satisfaction problem solver applet (with demo videos) that helps elucidate the basics of arc consistency and searching.

JS links

  • Constraint-Based Scheduling: An Introduction for Newcomers
    • PDF: A great overview of job scheduling problems and the use of constraint satisfaction techniques for solving them.
  • Time and Resource Constrained Scheduling: A Constraint Satisfaction Approach
    • Thesis: W. Nuijten's PhD thesis (Eindhoven University of Technology, 1994) provides an in-depth exploration of job scheduling problems and the constraint-based solution approach.
